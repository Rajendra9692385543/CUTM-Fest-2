Project Guidelines

Important rules and requirements for project submission and presentation.

Eligibility

Who Can Participate?

  • CUTM students (all years, primarily CSE and related branches)
  • Students from other universities and colleges
  • School students (Class 9-12)
  • Team size: 1-4 members
  • One project per team
Project Requirements

Technical Specifications

  • Working prototype or demo required
  • Source code must be available for review
  • Documentation (README, user manual)
  • Presentation slides (10-15 minutes)
  • Project should solve a real-world problem
Submission

What to Submit?

  • Project abstract (500 words max)
  • Technology stack details
  • Team member information
  • Faculty mentor details (if any)
  • Special requirements (power, internet, etc.)

Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated by academic and industry experts based on these criteria.

Innovation & Originality (25%)

Uniqueness of the idea, creative approach to problem-solving, and novelty of the solution.

Technical Implementation (25%)

Code quality, architecture design, use of appropriate technologies, and technical complexity.

Problem Statement (20%)

Clarity of the problem being solved, relevance to real-world issues, and market potential.

Presentation & Demo (20%)

Quality of presentation, effectiveness of demo, team coordination, and communication skills.

Documentation (10%)

Quality of code documentation, user manual, and project report.
